# About Me

I am a Master's student in the **Computer Graphics and Game Technology (CGGT)** program at the **University of Pennsylvania**. I previously received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from **Shenzhen University**.

# Research Interests

Basically everything about games and computer graphics, with some special interest in:
- **Real-Time Rendering:** Global Illumination, Sparse SDF, Physics Simulation, Shading
- **Graphics Programming:** Ray Tracing (DXR/Vulkan/CUDA), Procedural Generation
- **Game Technology:** Game Engines (Unreal/Unity), Neural Texture Compression
